# Contractor selection (risk-first)

Good selection reduces execution and funding risk.

Signals to capture:
- Number of relevant completed jobs
- Budget range experience (relevant budget)
- Review quality (granular categories: communication, schedule reliability, quality)
- Proof of insurance and licensing (if applicable)

Mitigations:
- Prefer contractors with relevant-job history
- Ask for a written scope + change order policy
- Use milestone-based payments with clear acceptance criteria
