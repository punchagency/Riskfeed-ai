# Permits and inspections (overview)

Most residential remodels require permits when you change structural, electrical, plumbing, or HVAC work.
Permits reduce risk by ensuring code compliance and documenting work.

Common risk signals:
- Work starts before permits are approved
- Repeated inspection failures
- Contractor cannot explain permit scope clearly

Mitigations:
- Confirm permit requirements with the local city/county
- Require a permit plan in the milestone schedule
- Keep inspection results attached to the milestone evidence
